The Challenge for Farmers & Consumers

Low Profits for Farmers

Middlemen and complex supply chains take a huge cut, leaving the farmers who do the work with minimal profit.

High Prices for Consumers

By the time produce reaches the city, multiple layers of commissions have inflated the price for everyday people.

Food Waste & Lack of Freshness

Each step in the long chain—transport, storage, and handling—leads to significant food loss and reduces freshness.

No Transparency

Customers have no connection to where their food comes from, and farmers have no connection to who buys their crops.

About AgriConnect

Our Mission

We're revolutionizing agriculture by connecting farmers directly with consumers, eliminating middlemen, and ensuring fair prices for everyone involved.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, AgriConnect started as a solution to the growing disconnect between farmers and consumers. We saw farmers struggling with low prices while consumers paid high prices for produce that had lost its freshness through long supply chains.

Our Impact

  • Increased farmer income by 60-80%
  • Reduced consumer prices by 20-30%
  • Decreased food waste by 30%
  • Connected 500+ farmers with 10,000+ consumers
